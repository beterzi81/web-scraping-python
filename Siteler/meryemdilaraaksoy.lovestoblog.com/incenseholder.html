INCENSE HOLDER

Materials

Polymer Clay, Water, Shaping tools

Making

- Firstly, we take a piece of clay and roll it and knead it until our hands get used to it.
- Then, we wet it with some water, roll it into a ball and smooth it with our hands.
- Next, we take our rolling pin and open our clay in a round shape.
- After that, we cut our dough with a round shape in the size we want.
- Then, we cut a short sized rectangular piece long enough to surround it and place it by wrapping it around the round piece.
- Next, we shape our dough into a tiny frog shape. We make legs and arms and glue them to the frog.
- After that, we glue our frog to the piece we made earlier.
- Then, we drill a tiny hole in the edge so that it can hold the incense stick.
- We leave our incense holder to dry on wax paper. It should dry within two days.
- Then, we paint our jewellery holder with acrylic paint in the color and pattern we want.
- Finally, we varnish our jewellery holder so that the paint does not come off and wear out.
- I am sure that it's amazing. Enjoy it!
