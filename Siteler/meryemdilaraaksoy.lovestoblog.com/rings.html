RINGS

Materials

Polymer Clay, Water, Shaping tools

Making

- Firstly, we take a piece of clay and roll it and knead it until our hands get used to it.
- Then, we wet it with some water, roll it into a ball and smooth it with our hands.
- Next, we take our rolling pin and open our clay in a round shape.
- After that, we cut the dough into thin long pieces and glue the parts we cut into rings.
- Then, we make tiny decorations in the shapes we want and stick them on the top of the ring.
- We leave our rings to dry on wax paper. They should dry within two days.
- Then, we paint our rings with acrylic paint in the color and pattern we want.
- Finally, we varnish our rings so that the paint does not come off and wear out.
- I am sure that they're amazing. Enjoy it!
