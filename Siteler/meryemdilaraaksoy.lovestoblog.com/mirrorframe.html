MIRROR FRAME

Materials

Polymer Clay, Water, Shaping tools

Making

- Firstly, we take a piece of clay and roll it and knead it until our hands get used to it.
- Then, we wet it with some water, roll it into a ball and smooth it with our hands.
- Next, we roll our dough with our hands and shape it into a snake.
- After curling and shaping our dough, we place it on our mirror and leave it to dry.
- Then, we paint our frame with acrylic paint in the color and pattern we want.
- Finally, we varnish our frame so that the paint does not come off and wear out.
