Guns N' Roses

Solo guitar performances, stage shows, unique music and much more must be the definition of this group. The band, whose discography is quite extensive, has many hit songs. The most popular ones are Estranged, November Rain, Sweet Child O' Mine, Paradise City... The list goes on. I can't help but mention Slash. A rock icon with his legendary solos and charisma. The group's November Rain I recommend listening to his solo in the piece. Guns N' Roses also holds the title of "World's The Most Dangerous Band".
