A Brief History of Rock Music


Rock music emerged in the second half of the 20th century. Influenced by the blues music genre. The blacks who invented the blues implicitly stated in their music the oppression they saw from the whites. As time progresses blues artists began to express their implicit expressions more openly and their music turned into a rebellion. started to enter. It was during this period that "rock" music began to make its name known. Especially in rock music, electric guitar The purpose of using it is because the raspy timbre of the sound effects reflects the rebellion and pain in the best way.
