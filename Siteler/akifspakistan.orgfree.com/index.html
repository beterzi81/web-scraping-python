A slight introduction of Pakistan:
Islamic Republic of Pakistan was founded on 14th August 1947, as a result of the never-ending efforts of Quaid E Muhammad Ali Jinnah, who is also known as the founder of Pakistan. Pakistan consists of 5 provinces. The capital city of Pakistan is Islamabad. Pakistan is located in South Asia and is neighbored by Iran, Afghanistan, India and China.
