Islamabad:The Capital of Pakistan
Islamabad, also known as the second most beautiful capital in the world, is the capital of Islamic Republic of Pakistan. It spreads up to an area of 906.5〖km〗^2.It was founded on 14th August,1967 and later replaced Karachi as the capital of Pakistan. This city is famous for its lush green atmosphere. Greenery is the most prominent feature of the city. Islamabad is surrounded by green hills from its two sides which is an extremely appealing site for the viewers. These are also used by its visitors for hiking and rock climbing. Coming to the history of the area on which the city is situated, the area has observed the Aryan settlements and the ancient caravans. Army of Alexander the Great is said to have passed from the area. Islamabad is one of the most visited cities in Pakistan. Being the capital city, the presence of all types of facilities is seen .
