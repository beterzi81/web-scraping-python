WHAT IS THE PLASTIC
MODELING



| Plastic modelling is shortly combining pieces of all sizes respectively and patiently, it is the step-by-step process of creating a miniaturized copy of the real in certain scales according the master modeller:
"It's not about creating masterpieces out of pieces that your mother would see while cleaning and throw away directly; It's fitting a tank or an airplane that can barely fit in a large hangar on a shelf in the house."
| Plastic models are vehicles or figures which are miniaturized to various sizes according to reference, made of plastic, disassembled and combinable with various tools.
